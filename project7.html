Video-Mapping Production

The following project was a collaboration with peers, to develop a video-mapping production. The production, had to relate to the portuguese revolution, and one of the prohibitions of the time. My peers and I selected the prohibition, "No Coca-Cola drinking". We used "Coca-Cola", as a symbol for freedom. As part of this project, we also had to develop a video-mapping company, and it's brand identity. This project was developed using, Blender 2, Adobe Premiere, Adobe After-effects, Adobe Illustrator.
