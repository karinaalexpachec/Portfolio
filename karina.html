About Me

I'm a Global Design student at IADE creative university, originally from Angola. For six years, I took the IB and DP design courses. Throughout these years, I sketched and designed products digitally, engaging in product design and prototyping. Eager to further develop my design skills with real-life applications and considerations, I chose to apply to the Global Design course at IADE. This has further fueled my passion for design and aspiration for a future career as a designer.
